About Us

Welcome to Parotta Cornar , where we bring the authentic flavors of South India to your plate. Established with a passion for culinary excellence, we take pride in serving one of the most beloved and cherished dishes of the region - the humble yet delectable parotta. At Parotta Cornar , we believe in the art of crafting parottas that are not only irresistibly flaky and flavorful but also made with the finest, handpicked ingredients. Our skilled chefs, steeped in the tradition of parotta-making, bring their expertise to the forefront, ensuring each bite is a symphony of taste and texture. We're more than just a parotta shop; we're a place where families and friends come together to savor moments of joy over plates of piping hot, freshly prepared parottas
